## Text

My best friend is Lisa. She is very important to me. We met in school when we were 10 years old. Since then, we have been inseparable. Lisa is kind, funny, and always helps me when I have problems.

Lisa is important to me because she listens. When I am sad or upset, I call her. She listens to my problems and gives me advice. She makes me feel better. Lisa knows how to make me laugh, even when I am having a bad day. Her jokes always cheer me up.

We have many hobbies in common. We both like to paint and we often spend weekends painting together. We also enjoy watching movies. Every Friday night, we have a movie night at my house. Lisa always brings popcorn, and we watch our favorite films.

I am grateful to have Lisa in my life because she is a true friend. She is honest and trustworthy. I can always count on her. When I need help with school work or personal issues, she is always there for me.

I hope we will be friends forever. Lisa is not just a friend; she is like family to me. She makes my life happier and more exciting. I am lucky to have her as my friend.
