In India, there is a special festival called Diwali. Diwali is the festival of lights. It is a happy time. People celebrate Diwali in October or November. Diwali lasts for five days.

On Diwali, families clean their homes. They decorate with small oil lamps called diyas. Many people also hang bright lights. Houses look beautiful and bright.

Friends and families give gifts to each other. They share sweets and special food. Children enjoy fireworks. They watch the colorful lights in the sky.

People wear new clothes on Diwali. They visit friends and family. It is a time for joy and togetherness.
