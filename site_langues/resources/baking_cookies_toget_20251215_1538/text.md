## Text

Baking cookies together is a fun and enjoyable activity that brings people closer. It's a perfect way to spend time with family or friends, especially on a rainy day. First, you need to gather all the ingredients like flour, sugar, butter, and chocolate chips. Everyone gets to participate in measuring the ingredients, which can be a bit messy but is always enjoyable.

Once everything is ready, the real fun begins. Mixing the dough is not only satisfying but also a great way to bond. Each person can take turns stirring the blend until it reaches the right consistency. You can try adding your own twist to the recipe, like some nuts or a splash of vanilla for extra flavor.

After the dough is ready, it's time to shape the cookies. You can make them into different shapes, using your hands or cookie cutters. It's amusing to see the creations everyone comes up with! Finally, you place them on a baking sheet and put them in the oven. The smell of cookies baking fills the house with warmth and happiness.

Waiting for them to bake is a good time to relax and talk. When they are done, the reward is delicious. Eating cookies fresh from the oven is the best part of the day. Baking together not only produces tasty treats but also creates lasting memories.
