## Prompts pour générer des textes de compréhension orale (200 mots, niveau très facile)

### Prompt 1 - Sunday Dinner with Grandma
Write a simple 200-word story in easy English about a teenager named Tom who visits his grandmother every Sunday for dinner. Describe their conversation about cooking traditional recipes. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), present tense, and everyday expressions. Include direct dialogue. Make it warm and family-oriented.

### Prompt 2 - Learning to Use a Smartphone
Write a simple 200-word dialogue in easy English between a young woman named Sarah who teaches her grandfather how to use his new smartphone. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), present tense, and basic technology words. Show patience and humor. Include step-by-step instructions that the grandfather finds confusing at first.

### Prompt 3 - Family Photo Album
Write a simple 200-word story in easy English about a 10-year-old girl named Emma who looks at old family photos with her great-grandmother. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), past simple tense for the stories in photos, and descriptive language. Include questions from Emma and memories from her great-grandmother.

### Prompt 4 - Gardening Together
Write a simple 200-word narrative in easy English about a grandfather teaching his teenage grandson about gardening on a Saturday morning. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), present continuous and imperatives. Include practical gardening tips and the grandson's reactions. Make it educational and heartwarming.

### Prompt 5 - Different Music Tastes
Write a simple 200-word dialogue in easy English between a grandmother and her teenage granddaughter discussing their different music preferences. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), present tense, and basic musical terms. Show respect and curiosity from both generations. Include examples of music genres.

### Prompt 6 - Birthday Party Planning
Write a simple 200-word story in easy English about three generations (grandmother, mother, and daughter) planning a family birthday party together. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), future tense with "going to", and everyday party vocabulary. Show different ideas and compromise. Make it cheerful.

### Prompt 7 - Recycling Habits
Write a simple 200-word conversation in easy English between a young boy named Jack who explains recycling to his grandfather. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), present simple tense, and basic environmental terms. Show the grandfather learning new habits. Keep the tone positive and encouraging.

### Prompt 8 - Bedtime Stories
Write a simple 200-word narrative in easy English about a grandfather who tells bedtime stories to his two young grandchildren every Friday night. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), present tense for the routine, and past tense for the story. Include the children's reactions and questions. Make it cozy and magical.

### Prompt 9 - Baking Cookies Together
Write a simple 200-word story in easy English about a grandmother and her 8-year-old granddaughter baking cookies together on a rainy afternoon. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), present continuous tense, cooking verbs, and sensory descriptions (smell, taste). Include small mistakes and laughter.

### Prompt 10 - Walking in the Park
Write a simple 200-word narrative in easy English about a teenage boy who takes his elderly neighbor for a walk in the park every weekend. Use simple vocabulary (A2-B1 level), present simple and present continuous. Describe what they see and talk about. Show kindness and friendship between generations. Keep the tone gentle and uplifting.
